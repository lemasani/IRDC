Research

The UDSM-RCFD Research Chair aims at conducting a demand drive, action oriented, gender transformative research which has impact on policy in four areas of displacement in East Africa base on the causes. These are (i) refugees and internally displaced person (IDPS) from conflicts; (ii) climate-disaster induced displaced (drought and floods); (iii) relocated or resettled population with little or unfair compensation to let for conservation or a development project; (iv) victims of trafficking and smuggling and other related population such as asylum seekers; mixed migrants and indigenous population and (v) the policy research (vi) and global policies and practices research (vii) collaboration with other Chairs funded by the IDRC in order to bring a global south voice into the displacement fora. Between July-December 2023 members conducted fieldwork and results were communicated in a symposium with government and non-governmental organization working on forced displacement in the country.
